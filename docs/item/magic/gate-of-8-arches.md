---
title: Gate of 8 Arches
---
One of the [Six Errant Gates](6-errant-gates.md), hidden within the structural supports of [L'Arc Chantante](../../place/landmark/building/ybbrinth/larc-chantante.md). This gate leads to [Quearn](../../place/planet/tirabol/quearn.md), the lost planet of the Beastfolk.
